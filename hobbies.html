Hobbies

Movies

I enjoy watching movies. I'm a fanatic of video production, so many movies out there appeal to me. It's very hard for me to choose which ones are my favorites, as I really go into the fictional world presented by each movie. However, here is a small list of the most notable movies I have watched.

Minions

The Minions movie is a very funny movie. Even though it is a movie for kids, it still appeals to adults as there are many funny innuendos targetting adults. Honestly, who can resist the adorable yellow minions?

The Tourist

This is a great movie because throughout the span of plot, the viewer perceives Clara Manzoni (Angelina Jolie) as a woman that is utilizing the dumb and helpless tourist Frank Tupeto (Johnny Depp) to distract the police from capturing her fugitive husband. However, as the movie progresses, we realize that Frank is a willing particpant and he is also anything but helpless and dumb. Both actors provide an excellent portrayal of the character's invovled.
